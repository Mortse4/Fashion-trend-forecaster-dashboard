Fashion Trends Are Endless

Fashion forecasting is the practice of predicting upcoming styles, silhouettes, colors, and consumer preferences. Its roots date back to the early 20th century when fashion houses began documenting collections and anticipating public tastes. Over time, this evolved into a global industry involving trend agencies, designers, and data analysts. With the rise of technology, forecasting has shifted from intuition and market observation to more data-driven approaches, using historical fashion cycles, social media trends, and now, machine learning and visual clustering. Today, forecasting blends creativity with computation to anticipate the next wave of style innovation.
